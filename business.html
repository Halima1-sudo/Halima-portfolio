Entrepreneurship

Rhazy Collection

Rhazy Collection is a modest fashion and halal skin care brand I founded to help women dress and care for themselves with both confidence and conviction.

As a passionate entrepreneur, I started Rhazy Collection because I kept noticing the same gap: women wanting fashion that respects their modesty without looking like an afterthought, and skin care they can trust to be halal without giving up on quality. Rhazy Collection sits right in that gap.

Modest Fashion

Thoughtfully designed pieces — hijabs, abayas, and modest everyday wear — made for women who refuse to choose between covering up and standing out.
