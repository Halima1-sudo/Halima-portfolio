About Me

The story behind the name

Replace with your photo

I'm Halima Yusuf Sani, a Level 2 Software Engineering student at Northwest University Kano, and the founder of Rhazy Collection.

I'm building my life around two things I care deeply about: understanding how software works, and helping women feel confident in what they wear and what they put on their skin. It might look like two different worlds, but to me they follow the same logic — start with a real problem, design something intentional, and keep improving it.

Through Rhazy Collection, I design modest wear and curate halal skin care for women who want style and self-care that align with their values. I'm a passionate entrepreneur, and every product I put out carries the same goal: helping women feel seen, respected, and cared for.

On the technical side, I'm early in my journey — currently comfortable with HTML and CSS, and holding a certificate in Responsive Web Design from freeCodeCamp. I'm patient with the process, because I know good work, whether it's a website or a wardrobe, is built one deliberate layer at a time.

How I Work

What guides me

Consistency

Whether it's a line of code or a line of stitching, I show up for the small repeated work that eventually becomes something whole.

Curiosity

I love reading and learning new things. It keeps both my code and my collections improving.

Modest fashion and halal skin care are not just products to me — they are a way of helping women carry themselves with confidence, on their own terms.
